Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Information collected

MSH Finance Group LLC collects information you choose to submit through website forms, including your name, email address, company name, location, service interests, message content, source page, consent selections, and whether you asked to receive email updates.

The website also uses a hidden bot-detection field on the contact form. Please do not fill out that field.

How information is used

MSH Finance Group LLC uses contact form information to respond to your inquiry, understand what services may be relevant, schedule a conversation, and determine the appropriate next step. MSH Finance Group LLC uses mailing-list information only when you choose to opt in to receive tax, accounting, compliance, and finance updates.

MSH Finance Group LLC does not sell your personal information and does not share contact form or mailing-list information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Form handling and service providers

Contact form submissions are processed through Netlify Forms, the website form-handling service used by this site. Netlify may store verified form submissions, make them available in the Netlify dashboard, include them in form-notification emails, and make them available through exports or account tools.

If you check the mailing-list box, MSH Finance Group LLC sends your name, email address, company name if provided, location, service interests, and related signup tags to Mailchimp for mailing-list management. Mailchimp may process that information to provide email marketing, audience management, unsubscribe, security, compliance, and service-administration features.

MSH Finance Group LLC uses these providers to operate the website, respond to inquiries, and manage email subscriptions. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy commitments. You can review Netlify privacy information at netlify.com/privacy and Mailchimp privacy information at intuit.com/privacy.

Mailing-list consent and unsubscribe

Mailing-list consent is optional and is not required to submit a contact inquiry. If you opt in, Mailchimp may send you a confirmation email before adding you to the mailing list. You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in Mailchimp emails or by contacting info@mshfinance.com.

What not to submit

Please do not submit sensitive personal or financial information through public website forms, including Social Security numbers, taxpayer identification numbers, bank information, login credentials, tax returns, full tax notices, or confidential documents. If sensitive information is needed, MSH Finance Group LLC will provide a secure portal link.

Retention and deletion

MSH Finance Group LLC keeps form submissions and mailing-list records only as long as reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, manage subscriptions, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and operate the website. You may request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance by emailing info@mshfinance.com. Please do not include sensitive identifiers in your request.

Analytics

No analytics tools are currently active on this site. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated to identify the tool and explain what it collects.

Privacy questions

For privacy questions, email info@mshfinance.com.
