Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Information collected

MSH Finance Group LLC collects the information you submit through the contact form or mailing-list opt-in, including your name, email address, company name, location, service interests, message content, and whether you asked to receive email updates.

How information is used

MSH Finance Group LLC uses contact form information to respond to your inquiry, understand what services may be relevant, schedule a conversation, and determine the appropriate next step. Contact form information is not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Mailing list

If you choose to join the mailing list, MSH Finance Group LLC may use your name and email address to send occasional tax, accounting, compliance, and finance updates. Mailing-list consent is optional and is not required to submit a contact inquiry. You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in an email or by contacting info@mshfinance.com.

What not to submit

Please do not submit sensitive personal or financial information through public website forms, including Social Security numbers, taxpayer identification numbers, bank information, login credentials, tax returns, full tax notices, or confidential documents. If sensitive information is needed, MSH Finance Group LLC will provide a secure portal link.

Analytics

No analytics tools are currently active on this site. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated to identify the tool and explain what it collects.

Privacy questions

For privacy questions, email info@mshfinance.com.

MSH Finance Group LLC

MSH Finance Group LLC is a tax, accounting and advisory firm. While individual members or employees of MSH Finance Group LLC may hold individual Certified Public Accountant (CPA) licenses in NY and/or NJ, MSH Finance Group LLC is not registered as a CPA firm in NJ and does not offer attest services (e.g. audits or review engagements).
