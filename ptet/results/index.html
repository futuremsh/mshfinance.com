Deadlines and payment cadence

Election and payment rules change. Treat these as general planning prompts and confirm current-year deadlines before acting.

  • NY PTET and NYC PTET require annual election and payment review.
  • NJ BAIT payments are generally reviewed around quarterly estimated payment dates.
  • Cash flow matters because entity-level tax payments can affect operating liquidity.

What to gather before a consult

  • Prior-year entity tax return.
  • Current-year P&L and balance sheet.
  • Owner residency details.
  • K-1s and ownership agreement.
  • Prior PTET/BAIT filings and estimated payments.
