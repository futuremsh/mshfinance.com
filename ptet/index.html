Who this is for

  • NY and NJ partnerships and S-corps.
  • Profitable owner-operated businesses.
  • Owners affected by the federal SALT cap.
  • Businesses with books current enough for planning.

What to gather

  • Prior-year entity return and K-1s.
  • Current-year P&L.
  • Owner residency information.
  • Estimated tax payments.
  • Prior PTET or BAIT filings, if any.

Common mistakes

  • Waiting until books are too stale to estimate income.
  • Ignoring owner residency and sourcing.
  • Making an election without cash-flow planning.
  • Treating a screening estimate as final tax advice.
