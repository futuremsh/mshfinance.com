Partner

Jacqueline Srour

Jacqueline brings a distinctive combination of tax, accounting and legal experience to MSH Finance Group LLC. She is a licensed CPA in New York and a licensed attorney in New York and New Jersey.

Her public accounting career includes time at Anchin, and her industry experience spans e-commerce and electronics retail.
