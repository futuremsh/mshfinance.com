Common questions

Do you only work with New York and New Jersey clients?

MSH works with clients across the country for fractional CFO and advisory services, bookkeeping, and federal tax matters. Our deepest expertise is in New York and New Jersey — we know the local tax landscape, compliance requirements, and cost structures inside out. For state and local tax compliance or tax resolution matters outside of NY or NJ, we're happy to consult and determine together whether we're the right fit.

Can you work with our existing systems and team?

Yes. Engagements are designed to integrate with current software and internal operations while improving controls and cadence.

Can you help with individual planning?

Yes. The review can cover take-home pay, withholding, rent burden, estimated tax planning, and business-owner questions.

Are you a CPA firm?

MSH Finance Group LLC is a tax, accounting and advisory firm. While individual members or employees of MSH Finance Group LLC may hold individual Certified Public Accountant (CPA) licenses in NY and/or NJ, MSH Finance Group LLC is not registered as a CPA firm in NJ and does not offer attest services (e.g. audits or review engagements).
