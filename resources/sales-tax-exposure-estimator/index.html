Risk indicators

Who this is for

  • Retailers, restaurants, cafes, salons, and contractors.
  • E-commerce and marketplace sellers.
  • Businesses that collected tax but may not have remitted it.
  • Businesses unsure what sales are taxable.

Common mistakes we see

  • Assuming software set up tax correctly.
  • Ignoring stale sales tax payable balances.
  • Not keeping resale certificates.
  • Letting multi-state sales go unreviewed.
What if I collected sales tax but did not remit it?

That can become serious quickly. Quantify the exposure and get advice before guessing.

What if I never registered?

Registration and cleanup options depend on facts, periods, jurisdictions, and whether notices have arrived.

Can I fix sales tax issues before an audit?

Often the first step is quantifying the issue and building a cleanup plan.

Why does QuickBooks sales tax payable matter?

A stale payable balance may indicate collected tax that has not been reconciled or remitted.
