What is PTET?

PTET generally refers to pass-through entity tax regimes that may affect entity and owner-level tax planning. Rules must be confirmed for the current year.

What is NJ BAIT?

NJ BAIT is New Jersey's pass-through business alternative income tax regime. It should be reviewed with entity facts and owner facts together.

Does every S-corp or partnership benefit?

No. Entity type, income, owners, estimates, cash flow, and state rules all matter.

Why do deadlines matter?

Election and payment timing can affect whether the planning is available or practical for a given year.
