Common mistakes we see

  • Ignoring the deadline.
  • Paying automatically without understanding the issue.
  • Responding without payment or filing records.
  • Waiting too long when collection language appears.
Should I ignore a notice if I think it is wrong?

No. Preserve the deadline and verify the facts before responding.

What if the deadline is soon?

A deadline within 14 days deserves urgent review.

What if I cannot pay the full balance?

Do not ignore it. Payment options and dispute options depend on the facts.

What if the notice is for sales or payroll tax?

Those categories can escalate quickly and should be reviewed promptly.
