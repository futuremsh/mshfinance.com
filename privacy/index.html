Privacy Policy

MSH Finance Group LLC uses a data-minimization approach. The public website should not collect SSNs, EINs, dates of birth, bank information, passwords, or confidential documents.

Last updated: May 10, 2026

What we collect

Public website forms may collect contact information such as name, email, phone number, business name, service interest, state, consent flags, source page, timestamp, and a short note if you provide one.

Lead-magnet calculators are designed to run in your browser. Calculation inputs are not intentionally stored server-side in v1. Some tools may store progress in your browser so you can view your result page.

What not to send through public forms

Do not submit SSNs, EINs, taxpayer IDs, dates of birth, full addresses, bank account numbers, login credentials, passwords, full tax notice scans, or confidential documents through public website forms.

Why we use information

  • To respond to consultation requests.
  • To send a requested report package if that feature is connected.
  • To send optional NYC/NJ tax tips only when you opt in.
  • To improve website usability and security.

Sharing and vendors

We do not sell your data. We may use vendors for website hosting, form handling, email delivery, spam prevention, analytics, or security. Vendors should be limited to what is needed to provide those services.

Email choices and unsubscribe

Marketing emails should include an unsubscribe link and a valid physical mailing address. Transactional report emails should not require newsletter consent and should not include promotional content for users who opted out.
